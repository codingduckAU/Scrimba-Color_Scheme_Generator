Color Scheme Generator (click for instructions)

1. Choose a colour from the colour-picker.
2. Select a scheme from the drop-down menu.
3. Click "Get color scheme" button.
4. Colour scheme will appear in the body.
5. Clicking on the hex values at the bottom, will copy it to the clipboard (desktop only)
